Wabi-Sabi Font Mockups

All recommended fonts + the 3 suggested heading/body pairings, shown with your earthy palette direction.

Alegreya

Quiet texture in every stroke

Useful for editorial headings and reflective brand voice. It feels bookish and warm without becoming ornate.

Cormorant Garamond

Elegant, weathered, and light

Strong for hero titles when you want restrained drama and a handcrafted sensibility.

EB Garamond

Timeless print-like calm

Great for longer reading blocks where softness and trust matter more than sharp modernity.

Fraunces

Old-style with personality

A display-forward serif that gives your headings a tactile, almost carved quality.

Spectral

Editorial but understated

Balanced enough for content-heavy pages while still retaining subtle character.

IBM Plex Sans

Neutral structure for UI

Works well for controls, menus, and body copy where clarity and rhythm are key.

Source Sans 3

Simple and unobtrusive

A practical companion to expressive serifs, especially for responsive interfaces.

Noto Sans JP

Clean with subtle restraint

Useful for multilingual contexts and for introducing Japanese typographic influence softly.

Zen Old Mincho

Traditional ink-like contrast and calm spacing

Use in selective headings or pull-quotes when you want a stronger cultural and poetic tone.
